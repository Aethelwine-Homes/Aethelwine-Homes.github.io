Frequently Asked Questions

Understanding how we work.

Do I need to own land first?+

No. Many clients begin the process without owning land. We can assist with sourcing a suitable stand as part of the initial project stages.

Can you help me find a stand?+

Yes. Stand sourcing assistance is one of our core services for clients who do not yet own vacant land.

Do you build the home?+

No. Aethel Homes is not a construction company or building contractor. We manage and coordinate the project on your behalf, while licensed builders carry out construction.

Who signs the construction contract?+

You do. You contract directly with your builder, architect, engineers and other consultants. Aethel Homes is appointed separately as your project manager.

Can I choose my own architect?+

Yes. You are welcome to appoint an architect of your choice, or we can recommend professionals from our independent network.

Can I choose my own builder?+

Yes. You retain the right to select your builder. We can assist in evaluating and comparing suitable candidates.

Can I customise my home?+

Absolutely. Every project is designed around your brief, working closely with your appointed architect to reflect your preferences.

How are project management fees charged?+

Fees are agreed upfront based on project scope and complexity, and are set out clearly in your project management agreement before work begins.
